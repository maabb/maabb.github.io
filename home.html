Welcome in Idlib



Idlib is a city located in the northwest of Syria
known as the green Idlib,because it is famous for its green fields and mild weather
Its neighbors are important cities like Aleppo and Hama;and it is bordering Turkey from the north ; so It is located as an important connecting area between Syrian cities and other countries..
In additional to that , It has a lot of cultural and economic components, It ranks first with the production of olive oil; also it includes all forms of the beautiful nature : forests, mountains,waterfalls, river and many water resources.
Despite its small size, it contains 15 olive oil soap factories and other industries which depend mainly on olive oil.
